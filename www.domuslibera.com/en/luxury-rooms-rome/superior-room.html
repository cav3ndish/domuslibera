Superior Room

    From the romantic and elegant style, our Superior Rooms of 28 m²  have a refined soul. The objects of great interior designers give character and refinement to the room, which is framed by marvelous exposed wooden beams.

 

    Inevitable, for us, is the satisfaction of our customers, so they can enjoy a flat-screen TV, the Wi-Fi connection and a direct dial telephone with unlimited calls: you will find everything you need to stay connected with the world  in the room.

 

    Actually, we want you to feel at home in our rooms: you can take a coffee break in a relaxing ambience, thank to a Nespresso coffee machine and a tè kettle that waiting you in the room.

 

    The comfort, prince of our rooms, translates into King Size beds and airy and spacious bathrooms with a rainfall showers and everything you need for your stay: piles of fluffy towels, hairdryer and bottles of beautifully fragranced toiletres.
